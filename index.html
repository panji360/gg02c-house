Information: House


As a fictional city set around 13th Century Java, a house in Gagelang would follow the style of Majapahit house. Majapahit houses were arranged in groups surrounded by brick walls.


These houses would have stone foundations, roofs decorated with curly corners (“ukel”), walls from woven bamboo slats, and brick floors. Two small stairs would lead the occupants through the door.


The architectural style of these houses can still be seen in traditional Bali houses today, since they were influenced by Majapahit architecture.
